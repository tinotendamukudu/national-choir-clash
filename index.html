About Us

At the National Clash of Churches/Choir Zimbabwe, we believe in the power of music to unite communities and inspire spiritual growth. This prestigious event brings together choirs from churches across the nation, celebrating the diversity of worship through gospel music. Our mission is to provide a platform for Zimbabwean church choirs to showcase their musical excellence, foster unity, and uplift souls through powerful performances.
