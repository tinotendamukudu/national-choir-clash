General frequently asked questions

Have Any Question ?

Any registered church choir from Zimbabwe can participate. Choirs must represent their church and follow the submission guidelines.

To register, simply fill out the registration form on our website and upload your choir's performance video.

The competition consists of two rounds: the Gold Round, where choirs submit videos and the public votes online, and the Platinum Round, where the top 10 choirs perform live at HICC before a panel of judges.

After all video submissions are received, they will be posted on the website for public voting. Supporters can vote for their favorite choirs during the Gold Round.
